Support Ukrainians Stuck in Hungary

Overview

A new law has come into force in Hungary which threatens to push thousands of Ukrainian refugees onto the streets or back to Ukraine. More than 30,000 Ukrainians are stuck in Hungary and need our help.

For many, the change will result in job losses and impact school enrolment, jeopardizing the positive integration achievements obtained so far.

The solution is to relocate them to Canada through the Canada-Ukraine Authorization for Emergency Travel (CUAET) program. This project aims to provide support to Ukrainians currently stranded in Hungary and assist them in their journey to Canada through the CUAET program. We are dedicated to helping those in need during these challenging times.

About the CUAET Program

The Canada-Ukraine Authorization for Emergency Travel (CUAET) program allows Ukrainians to travel to Canada temporarily during the ongoing crisis. This initiative provides a pathway for those in need of urgent protection and safety.

Benefits of the CUAET Program:

  • Temporary Residency: Ukrainians can stay in Canada for up to three years, providing them with a safe haven during the crisis.
  • Access to Services: Beneficiaries can access health care, education, and other essential services during their stay in Canada.
  • Work Opportunities: The program allows individuals to work in Canada, helping them support themselves and contribute to their new community.
  • Family Reunification: Eligible individuals can include family members in their application, facilitating family reunification and support.
  • Pathway to Permanent Residency: The program may offer pathways to permanent residency for those who wish to remain in Canada long-term.
  • Financial Aid: The program provides financial assistance of up to $3,500 per family to help with relocation expenses.

For many Ukrainians, the CUAET program has been a lifeline, successfully helping thousands find safety and a new beginning in Canada. Our team of immigration lawyers and experts is dedicated to providing personalized support to ensure a smooth relocation process. We offer comprehensive services including application assistance, legal advice, and guidance on integrating into Canadian society. Our expertise is crucial in navigating the complexities of the program and maximizing the chances of a successful relocation.

For more information about the CUAET program, visit the official Canadian government page.
