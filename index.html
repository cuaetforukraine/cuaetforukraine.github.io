Welcome to Ukrainians Relocation to Canada

We help Ukrainians in Hungary to relocate to Canada through the CUAET program. This program offers a pathway to work and eventually permanent residency in Canada for eligible Ukrainian citizens.

About Us

We are a non-profit organization dedicated to helping Ukrainians in Hungary to relocate to Canada through the CUAET program. Our team of experts provide guidance and support every step of the way, from application preparation to settlement in Canada.

CUAET Program

The CUAET (Canada-Ukraine Authorization for Emergency Travel) program is a unique initiative by the Canadian government to help Ukrainian citizens in Hungary and other European countries to come to Canada as temporary workers. The program offers a streamlined application process and work permits that can be extended, leading to permanent residency.

Key benefits of the CUAET program include:

  • Work permit: Eligible Ukrainian citizens can apply for an open work permit, which allows them to work in any occupation and for any employer in Canada.
  • Eventual permanent residency: After working in Canada for a certain period, eligible applicants can apply for permanent residency.
  • Access to healthcare and education: CUAET program participants have access to Canadian healthcare and education systems, ensuring a smooth transition to their new life in Canada.
  • Faster processing times: The CUAET program offers faster processing times compared to regular work permit applications, allowing Ukrainians to start their new life in Canada sooner.
  • Financial aid: The CUAET program offers financial aid. families are expected to receive CAN$3,000 for each adult (18 or older) and CAN$1,500 for each child (17 and under)
